OBJECTIVES

COSMIC UNIT BUYERS ASSOCIATION( CUBA) is formed and Registered to provide welfare,
cooperation in development of associations in the projects of Cosmic group, and for the said
purpose to create a platform for awareness amongst members about their rights, duties and
responsibilities in the said projects. CUBA is Recognized Consumer Welfare Association,
with sole aim to protect the interest of the Allot tees of the Units of various projects
of Cosmic Group.
In view of ongoing Crisis where people hard earned money is at Stake and all Cosmic
projects are stalled , Association takes on the additional responsibility to expedite and work
towards revival of projects by participating in legal course as a positive voice of Allottees who
want possession of their units with all promises/commitments that were given during booking.
So it is time for all of us to come
forward and join hands so that Honorable Courts recognizes our voice and give all of us a solution
where developer or Co-developer are given directions to complete the projects in time bound manner.

You becoming a member of CUBA will be first step towards positivity.
